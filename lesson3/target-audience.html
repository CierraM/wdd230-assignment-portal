Target Audience

The audience for this website would include people living in the area of the weather forecast who are interested in knowing the upcoming weather. These people probably spend at least some of their day outdoors.

Persona #1:

  • Jonathan Beach
  • Jonathan is a potato farmer. He spends a lot of time outside working on his farm. He is 35 years old with a wife and two kids. He is a bishop of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints.
  • "How cold is it going to get this week? I need to make sure my crops won't freeze."
  • "Is it going to rain during the ward party this weekend?"

Persona #2:

  • Emily Brown
  • Emily is a 10th grade high school student. She often rides her bike to school. She has three younger siblings. In her free time, she likes to hang out with her friends.
  • "Is it going to be too cold tomorrow for me to take my bike to school?"
  • "Is it sunny enough this week to melt all the snow?"
  • "Should I do an indoor or outdoor activity with my friends tomorrow?"
