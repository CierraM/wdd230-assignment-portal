Hick's Law

Tesla

tesla.com

Tesla's website exemplifies Hick's law with their very minimalist design. There are as few buttons as absolutely necessary, eliminating choices and making it easier for the user to decide what they want.

Alignment

Etsy

etsy.com

Etsy's website is held together by the use of alignment. Although there are many different products displayed on the home page, the margins are consistent, and everything is blocked out in a way that keeps the design clean.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

Apple's website makes good use of white space. Though there is plenty of text, the design is clean because of the space around it.
